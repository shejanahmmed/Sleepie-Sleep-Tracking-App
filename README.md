## 📖 Overview

**Sleepie** is a fully offline, high-precision sleep posture tracking and sound-profiling application. It turns your smartphone's hardware sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope, and microphone) into a clinical-grade tracking device without sending a single byte of your data to the cloud.

Designed with an ultra-minimalist, high-contrast, dot-matrix visual interface, Sleepie operates in both unified Light and Dark modes. The app classifies your posture in real time, logs snoring events, and evaluates your overall sleep quality using a custom scientific formula.

## 📐 Technical Deep-Dive: Posture Classification Math

Sleepie relies on an elegant, energy-efficient, rule-based classification algorithm (`RuleBasedPostureClassifier`) that computes device tilt angles directly from raw IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit) data. This eliminates the battery drain and performance overhead associated with deep learning models, keeping tracking completely localized and lightweight.

### 1. Orientation Mathematics

The classifier captures 3-axis acceleration ($a_x, a_y, a_z$) and angular velocity ($\omega_x, \omega_y, \omega_z$) to compute **Pitch** and **Roll** angles:

* **Pitch** (tilt forward/backward along the longitudinal axis):
  $$\theta = \arctan2\left(a_y, \sqrt{a_x^2 + a_z^2}\right) \times \frac{180}{\pi}$$

* **Roll** (tilt left/right along the transverse axis):
  $$\phi = \arctan2\left(-a_x, a_z\right) \times \frac{180}{\pi}$$

* **Gyroscope Magnitude** (rotational movement intensity):
  $$\omega_{\text{mag}} = \sqrt{\omega_x^2 + \omega_y^2 + \omega_z^2}$$

### 2. Decision Boundaries & Rules

At each 15-second tracking interval, the sensor values are parsed using the following hierarchical state boundaries:

* **Movement State (`MOVING`)**: If the gyroscope magnitude exceeds the baseline movement threshold:
  $$\omega_{\text{mag}} > 0.65 \text{ rad/s}$$
* **Side Sleeping (`SIDE`)**: If the roll angle indicates the device is tilted on its edge:
  $$35^{\circ} < |\phi| < 135^{\circ}$$
* **Stomach Sleeping (`STOMACH`)**: If the device is lying flat face-down, or tilted significantly forward:
  $$|\phi| \ge 135^{\circ} \quad \text{or} \quad |\theta| > 45^{\circ}$$
* **Back Sleeping (`BACK`)**: If the device is lying relatively flat screen-up:
  $$|\phi| \le 35^{\circ} \quad \text{and} \quad |\theta| \le 45^{\circ}$$

## 🎙️ Acoustic Snore Profiling & Positional Therapy

### 🔊 On-Device Audio Sampling
The app uses `AudioRecord` to sample audio at **16 kHz** (mono, 16-bit PCM). The audio data is processed strictly in a local memory buffer to compute the real-time amplitude value (RMS). No audio recordings are ever written to the disk or sent over a network. 
* If the processed amplitude exceeds a calibrated limit (`SNORE_AMPLITUDE_THRESHOLD`), the current 15-second epoch is marked as a snoring event.

### ⚡ Smart Alert Engine (Positional Therapy)
Sleeping flat on your back (`BACK` posture) relaxes throat muscles and narrows the airway, which is the leading cause of positional snoring and obstructive sleep apnea. 
* When the **Alert Engine** detects snoring while the user is in the `BACK` position, it triggers a progressive series of gentle, custom vibration patterns.
* This haptic feedback acts as a subconscious cue, training the body to roll over to a healthy side posture (`SIDE`) without fully waking the user.
* Alert thresholds and vibration intensity can be customized in the settings.

## 📊 Sleep Scoring Algorithm

At the end of a session, a custom sleep index score (0-100) is calculated combining three weighted components:

$$\text{Sleep Score} = \left( W_{\text{duration}} \times S_{\text{duration}} \right) + \left( W_{\text{posture}} \times S_{\text{posture}} \right) + \left( W_{\text{snore}} \times S_{\text{snore}} \right)$$

* **Duration Score ($S_{\text{duration}}$)**: Linear score based on target sleep hours (ideal: 7-8 hours).
* **Posture Stability Score ($S_{\text{posture}}$)**: Evaluated by measuring the number of posture shifts. High shifts suggest tossing, turning, and restless sleep, which reduces this score.
* **Snoring Penalty ($S_{\text{snore}}$)**: Computed by dividing snoring epochs by total tracked epochs, scaling down the score as snoring frequency and average amplitude rise.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture

3. Let Gradle sync complete successfully.
4. Clean and compile the project using Gradle CLI or Android Studio:
   ```bash
   ./gradlew assembleDebug
   ```
5. Deploy the debug build to your physical device or emulator.

## 🛌 Best Practices for Accurate Tracking

Since Sleepie calculates posture based on relative spatial orientation:
1. **Device Placement**: Place the phone flat on the mattress or bed next to you (e.g., parallel to your body, close to chest level). Placing the phone on a nightstand will make posture classification impossible.
2. **Device Orientation**: The top of the phone should face the head of the bed.
3. **Power Management**: Ensure the app is excluded from Android’s aggressive app standby or battery saving settings to prevent background service termination:
   * Go to **Settings** -> **Apps** -> **Sleepie** -> **Battery** -> Select **Unrestricted**.
4. **Permissions**: Allow **Microphone** permission (required for Snore detection) and **Notification** permission (required for the Foreground tracking service).

## 🔒 Privacy & Local Security

* **100% Local**: No network permissions are registered in the manifest. The app operates fully sandboxed offline.
* **Transient Audio Analysis**: Audio records are processed immediately to calculate volume levels and instantly discarded. The app never records or saves your voice.
* **Open Source Database**: The tracker's sqlite DB is stored securely in the app's private sandbox and is completely wiped if the app is uninstalled.
